When the mood for watching a movie strikes, but you have nothing to watch

Filmis is here to save you from the dreadful activity of having to first find a movie to watch alone or with others and secondly where to watch it. Without Filmis, you almost lose your mood for watching a movie since you have to go back and forth among numerous apps, movie lists, IMDB and critics' scores until you are finally satisfied with your choice. A smooth ride from when the mood strikes to actually hitting play - FILMIS the APP!
